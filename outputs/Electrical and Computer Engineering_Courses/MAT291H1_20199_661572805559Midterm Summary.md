Midterm Summary 
 
The following gives a brief description of the content of the midterm. No further information will 
be provided. 
q1 20 marks (3 parts):  limits, partial differentiation and chain rule, tangent plane 
q2 20 marks (1 part): Lagrange multipliers 
q3 30 marks (3 parts): double integral in cartesian coordinates, double integration in polar 
coordinates, triple integral in cartesian coordinates 
q4 30 marks (2 parts): integration in spherical coordinates, double integration with a change of 
variables
